# 🗂️ Reverse Engineering in MySQL Workbench (Steps)

1. Click on **Database** in the top navbar

2. Select **Reverse Engineer**

3. Click **Next**

4. Choose your **connection (database)**

5. Click **Next**

6. Select the **schema/database** you want

7. Click **Next**

8. Select the **tables** you want to include in the EER diagram

9. Click **Next → Next → Finish**

10. The **EER Diagram (canvas with tables)** will be generated

11. View the table relationships

12. Close the tab after viewing

---

# ⚡ One-Line Summary

👉 Reverse Engineering converts an existing database into an **EER diagram (visual structure)**
